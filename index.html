Welcome

Every family leaves behind more than names and dates.

They leave stories, photographs, traditions, hardships, laughter, places, objects, and memories. This museum preserves those gifts for future generations.

Begin at the center—with the living family—and allow the redwood to grow outward one verified generation at a time.
